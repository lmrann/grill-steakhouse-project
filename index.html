Welcome to Grill SteakHouse

About Us

Grill SteakHouse has been the ultimate destination for steak lovers of Dhaka. We take immense pride in being the most popular restaurant for steaks. Our commitment to delivering the tastiest steaks combined with a luxurious environment has earned us the reputation of being a go-to destination for both locals and visitors alike.

Our Story

The Steak House was founded in 2016 with a simple yet ambitious vision to provide the people of Dhaka with an unforgettable dining experience. From day one, we set out to redefine the steak experience in Bangladesh, and our relentless dedication to quality and excellence has been the key to our success.

Secret of our Steaks

At the heart of our success lies the exceptional quality of our steaks. We source only the finest cuts of meat from trusted suppliers, ensuring that every bite is tender, juicy, and bursting with flavor. Our skilled chefs masterfully craft each steak to perfection to guarantee an unforgettable dining experience.
